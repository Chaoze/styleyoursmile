Invisalign in Zurich

Clear aligners for a straighter smile - without metal braces, comfortable and discreet.

What is Invisalign?

Invisalign uses transparent aligners instead of visible metal brackets. These aligners are custom-made for you and guide your teeth gently and precisely into the desired position. Thanks to modern 3D technology, each step of the treatment can be planned in advance before it even begins.

As a certified Invisalign provider in Zurich, we support you from the first consultation to the final result. The clear trays are almost invisible, easy to remove and fit smoothly into everyday life.

When are aligners a good option?

Invisalign can be used for a wide range of orthodontic corrections. Typical indications include:

  • Crowding
  • Gaps between teeth
  • Crossbite
  • Open bite
  • Deep bite

In a personal consultation, our certified specialists will assess whether Invisalign is the right solution for your individual situation. Many corrections that previously required fixed braces can now be treated comfortably with aligners.

How treatment works

  1. Initial consultation with certified specialists
  2. Digital imaging and impressions
  3. Virtual 3D preview of the treatment plan
  4. Delivery of the first aligner sets
  5. Check-ups every 4 weeks

Your advantages

Invisalign offers a range of advantages that make orthodontic treatment much easier in everyday life:

  • Nearly invisible appearance
  • Comfortable smooth plastic material
  • Removable for eating and cleaning
  • Better oral hygiene during treatment
  • Predictable results thanks to 3D technology

Frequently asked questions about Invisalign

Treatment time depends on the complexity of the correction and is typically between 6 and 18 months. Mild cases can often be completed more quickly. We will give you an individual estimate during your first consultation.

Costs vary depending on the scope of treatment and the number of aligner sets required. After the consultation and digital analysis, we provide a transparent individual quote without hidden costs.

For the best results, we recommend wearing the aligners for at least 20 to 22 hours per day. You can simply remove them for meals, drinks other than water and daily oral hygiene.

Basic insurance usually does not cover Invisalign. Some supplementary insurance plans may contribute part of the cost. We are happy to discuss financing options during your consultation.
