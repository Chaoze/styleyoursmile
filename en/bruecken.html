Dental bridges in Zurich

Replace missing teeth and close gaps reliably.

What are dental bridges?

A dental bridge is a fixed restoration used to close one or more missing-tooth gaps. The bridge is supported by the neighbouring teeth on either side of the gap, known as abutment teeth. The replacement teeth in the middle are called pontics.

Unlike a removable denture, a bridge is permanently fixed. It feels stable, functions much like a natural tooth and does not need to be removed for cleaning.

Bridge vs. implant — which option fits better?

Implants are often considered the most independent solution because they preserve bone and do not require preparation of neighbouring teeth. A bridge, however, remains a proven and often faster or more economical option, especially if the adjacent teeth already carry crowns or if implant placement is not the right choice. We advise you individually on which approach makes most sense for your situation.

When is a bridge a good option?

A dental bridge can be an excellent solution in the following situations:

  • One or more missing teeth

    Bridges can replace a single missing tooth or, if the supporting teeth are suitable, several consecutive missing teeth.

  • Stable neighbouring teeth are present

    The abutment teeth need to be healthy and strong enough to carry the bridge long term. In some cases they are restored with crowns first.

  • An alternative to implants is preferred

    If implant treatment is not ideal for medical, anatomical or financial reasons, a bridge offers a reliable fixed alternative.

Materials

We offer dental bridges in different materials, matched to your aesthetic expectations, the position in the mouth and your budget:

Best aesthetics

All-ceramic

Metal-free ceramic with excellent translucency. Ideal for visible teeth and a natural-looking smile.

High strength

Porcelain-fused-to-metal

A metal framework with a ceramic outer layer. A proven option for back teeth with long clinical experience.

Modern & durable

Zirkon

High-strength zirconia combines the aesthetics of ceramic with the resilience needed for demanding chewing forces.

The best material depends on the location of the gap, the functional demands and your personal priorities. We explain the options clearly and recommend what genuinely suits you.

Treatment process

Bridge treatment usually takes place over two appointments and typically follows these steps:

  1. Consultation and diagnosis We assess the situation in your mouth, evaluate the supporting teeth and discuss the right material and treatment plan with you, including a written estimate.
  2. Preparation of the abutment teeth The neighbouring teeth are shaped carefully so the bridge can fit precisely. A temporary bridge protects the area during fabrication.
  3. Digital scan Using an intraoral scanner, we create a highly precise digital impression that is fast, comfortable and free from conventional impression material.
  4. Labor fabrication The bridge is produced with great precision according to our specifications. In selected cases, production in our in-house CAD/CAM workflow is possible.
  5. Fitting and final check The finished bridge is tried in, checked for fit, aesthetics and bite, and then definitively bonded. We also guide you through aftercare.
