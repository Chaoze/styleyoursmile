Teeth whitening in Zurich

Professional whitening for a brighter smile - gentle, effective and long-lasting.

What is professional whitening?

Professional whitening is one of the most effective ways to brighten discoloured teeth without harming the enamel. Unlike over-the-counter products, we use high-quality whitening agents selected to suit your teeth and remove deeper stains more reliably.

Discolouration can be caused by everyday factors such as coffee, tea and red wine, which leave pigments in the enamel, as well as smoking and the natural ageing process. Professional bleaching can address these changes in a controlled and gentle way.

Your advantages

Professional whitening at the dentist offers important advantages compared with home products alone:

  • Noticeable whitening by several shades, often visible after one session
  • Removal of persistent staining from coffee, tea, red wine and nicotine
  • Gentle approach with professional protection for gums and enamel
  • Longer-lasting results thanks to clinically controlled products
  • More confidence through a brighter and natural-looking smile

Our whitening methods

Depending on your wishes and the starting situation, we offer different whitening options:

In-office whitening

The treatment is performed directly at the practice. A highly effective whitening gel is applied to the teeth and activated under controlled conditions. In around 40 to 60 minutes, a clearly brighter result can often be achieved.

Home whitening

Custom trays are filled with a lower-concentration whitening gel and worn daily for a limited time. The result develops gradually over 1 to 2 weeks and offers a particularly flexible option at home.

Power whitening

An intensified in-office approach for more pronounced whitening in a single session, especially when discoloration is stronger.

How long does the result last?

The longevity of the result depends on your habits. In many cases, the brighter shade can last 1 to 4 years. Frequent coffee, tea, red wine consumption or smoking usually means that refreshers may be needed sooner.

With a home maintenance set, the result can often be refreshed from time to time after the initial treatment.

Tips for keeping teeth brighter for longer

These measures help support your whitening result:

  • Brush thoroughly twice a day with a soft toothbrush and fluoride toothpaste
  • Use gentle whitening toothpaste without overly abrasive ingredients
  • Reduce staining foods and acidic drinks where possible
  • Attend regular hygiene visits, for example every 6 months, to remove new deposits early
