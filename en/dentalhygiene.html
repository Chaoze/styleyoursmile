Dental hygiene in Zurich

Professional teeth cleaning from CHF 140 for healthy teeth and fresher breath.

What is dental hygiene?

Dental hygiene includes much more than a standard cleaning. It is a comprehensive preventive concept designed to maintain healthy teeth and gums and to help avoid more complex dental problems over time.

Key elements include the removal of tartar and hard deposits that cannot be eliminated by brushing alone, support in cases of gum inflammation and the prevention of periodontal disease. Our qualified dental hygienists Daniela Graf-Wiegand, Lucy Ann Oakley and Nadia Corona carry out every treatment with care and modern instruments.

Professional teeth cleaning from CHF 140. The final cost depends on the individual effort required. Feel free to contact us for a personal estimate or book online directly.

Your advantages

Regular dental hygiene is one of the most effective measures for long-term oral health:

  • Helps reduce the risk of caries and periodontal disease
  • Supports keeping your natural teeth for as long as possible
  • Teeth look cleaner and often brighter
  • Fresh and clean feeling after each session
  • Can help reduce bad breath

How treatment works

A dental hygiene session at StyleYourSmile usually takes around one hour and follows a structured process:

  1. Assessment and advice - Your dental hygienist first evaluates your gums and teeth.
  2. Removal of hard deposits - Tartar and deposits above and below the gum line are removed carefully using ultrasonic and hand instruments.
  3. Cleaning between the teeth - Floss, interdental brushes and airflow technology help clean areas that are difficult to reach.
  4. Polishing - Professional polishing smooths the tooth surface and makes it harder for new deposits to adhere.
  5. Fluoride and home-care advice - The treatment is completed with fluoride protection and tailored recommendations for daily oral care.

The session usually lasts about one hour. If treatment needs are higher, a second appointment may be advisable.

How often should you come for dental hygiene?

We usually recommend professional cleaning every 3 to 6 months, depending on your personal risk profile. Patients with increased periodontal risk, smokers or people with diabetes often benefit from more frequent visits.

Basic health insurance usually does not cover dental hygiene, but supplementary insurance may contribute depending on your policy.
