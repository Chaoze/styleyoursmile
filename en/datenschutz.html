2. Collection and processing of personal data

Website access data (server logs)

When you visit our website, technical access data is automatically recorded by the web server, such as IP address, date and time of access, requested page, transferred data volume, browser type and operating system. This data is used exclusively for technical security and error analysis and is generally stored for a maximum of 3 months.

Contact form

If you contact us via the contact form or by email, the data you provide will be used solely to process your request and will be deleted once the request has been completed, unless a legal retention obligation applies.

Appointments

Appointments are managed via SimplyBook.it. The data collected there is used for practice organisation and patient administration. Appointment-related data is generally deleted 12 months after the last patient contact unless longer statutory retention periods apply.

3. Third-party services

Google Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google LLC. Google Analytics uses cookies to analyse website usage. We use IP anonymisation where applicable. You can disable tracking with the browser add-on provided by Google.

Google Maps

We use Google Maps to display the location of our practice. When using the map, data including your IP address may be transmitted to Google.

Cloudflare

Our website is protected by the content delivery network and security services of Cloudflare. In this context, IP addresses and technical data may be processed temporarily.

4. Cookies

This website uses cookies. Technically necessary cookies are required for the operation of the website. Analysis cookies are used only with your consent where applicable. You can deactivate or delete cookies in your browser settings at any time.

5. Your rights

Under Swiss data protection law, you have rights including access, rectification, deletion, restriction of processing, data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with the Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner.

  • Access: request information about stored personal data
  • Rectification: request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: request deletion where no legal retention obligation applies
  • Restriction: request limitation of processing in certain cases
  • Data portability: request your data in a common machine-readable format
  • Complaint: contact the FDPIC at www.edoeb.admin.ch

To exercise your rights, please contact us in writing at info@styleyoursmile.ch.

6. Data security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss or misuse. Sensitive data is transmitted in encrypted form via HTTPS/TLS.

7. Changes to this privacy policy

We reserve the right to adapt this privacy policy when necessary. The current version is always available on this page. Status: February 2025.
