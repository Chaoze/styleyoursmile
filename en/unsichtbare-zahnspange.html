Invisible braces - straighter teeth

Everything about Invisalign and clear aligners - discreet, comfortable and removable.

What is Invisalign?

Invisalign is one of the best-known systems for correcting teeth with transparent aligners. Individually fabricated clear trays are worn over the teeth and guide them step by step into the planned position. The treatment is digitally planned, and in many cases the expected result can be visualised before treatment starts.

Advantages compared with classic metal braces

  • Almost invisible: the clear material is barely noticeable in everyday life.
  • Removable: aligners can be taken out for meals and oral hygiene.
  • Comfortable: no brackets or wires that rub against the lips and cheeks.
  • Easy hygiene: brushing and flossing remain straightforward.
  • Digitally planned: the treatment pathway can be simulated in advance.

Typical treatment steps

  1. Initial consultation: we assess whether clear aligners are suitable for your situation.
  2. Digital scan: your teeth are recorded precisely without conventional impression material.
  3. Treatment planning: the movements are planned in a structured sequence.
  4. Active treatment: aligners are changed regularly and progress is reviewed at control appointments.
  5. Retention: retainers help stabilise the result after treatment.

Treatment time

Treatment time depends on the starting position and the desired correction. Mild cases are often shorter, while more complex movements take longer. Wearing the aligners consistently is essential for good results.

What can clear aligners correct?

  • Mild to moderate crowding
  • Gaps between teeth
  • Certain bite deviations
  • Relapse after previous orthodontic treatment

When fixed braces may be more suitable

In more complex cases, or when tooth and jaw relationships require stronger control, fixed orthodontic appliances may still be the more appropriate option. We will advise you honestly on which approach makes the most sense in your case.

Costs and insurance

Costs depend on the complexity of treatment. In Switzerland, clear aligner treatment is usually a private service, though some supplementary insurance plans may contribute depending on the policy. We provide a transparent cost overview before treatment begins.

Personal Invisalign consultation

We will show you which options are realistic for your teeth and whether clear aligners are the right fit for your treatment goals.
